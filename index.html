Our Story: A Mission Driven by Service and Safety

The Lark & Lavender Luxury Group, INC. (LLLG) and its operating division, the Luxe Bus Co., were founded not just as a business, but as a direct response to critical community needs. We are a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Business (SDVOB), bringing the military values of integrity, precision, and mission-focus to every mile we drive.

From Active Duty to Active Community Service

Our story is rooted in the commitment to serve. Founded by U.S. Veteran Byron Jackson and woman-leader Kishami Jackson, LLLG represents a powerful fusion of military discipline and compassionate entrepreneurship.

Byron Jackson’s military service instilled a non-negotiable standard for mission completion, logistics management, and safety protocols—principles that now govern the operation of every vehicle in the Luxe Bus Co. fleet. After witnessing systemic transportation gaps in our community, we saw an opportunity to apply this high-level discipline to civilian life.

Kishami Jackson brings the vision and operational expertise, ensuring that our services are not only reliable but are delivered with a level of care and luxury that treats every client with the utmost respect.

Together, we built Lark & Lavender Luxury Group INC. DBA Lark and Lavender Luxe Bus CO on the belief that access to safe, reliable, and specialized transportation, and delivery service, should never be a barrier to opportunity.

The Dual Mission: Safety, Access, and Reliability

The Luxe Bus Co. is dedicated to addressing the most significant transportation challenges in New York State through two core phases:

Phase 1: Current Focus - Premium Local Delivery and Logistics
CURRENTLY OPERATIONAL: We are leveraging our fleet and logistics expertise to provide premium, reliable, and time-sensitive delivery solutions for local businesses and organizations. This initial focus allows us to establish operational infrastructure, build relationships, and maintain the highest safety standards, all while preparing for the launch of our full passenger services.

Phase 2: Launching February 2026 - Specialized Passenger Transport
COMING FEBRUARY 2026: We are committed to using our full fleet capacity to solve critical passenger access needs. Our expansion will focus on two key areas:

  • Addressing the Child Care Desert Crisis: Providing premium, certified transportation solutions for children to and from licensed daycares and learning centers, helping working families overcome logistics barriers.
  • Specialized Veteran and Disabled Adult Transport: Contracting with state agencies like OPWDD and the DVA to provide crucial access for Veterans and Disabled Young Adults to medical appointments, job training, day programs (ACCES-VR), and independent living activities.

This commitment is supported by our non-profit arm, the Tea Party With a Purpose Foundation, which works to foster community and support for these very same populations.

Why Choose a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Business?

  • Integrity: We operate with complete transparency and ethical conduct, a standard derived from our military roots.
  • Uncompromising Safety: Our logistics and fleet maintenance programs are managed with the rigorous precision of an operational command.
  • Mission-Driven: Our success is measured by the community impact we make—not just the miles we cover.

We are not only building a business with good care and customer service—we are building a legacy. One Community at a time.
